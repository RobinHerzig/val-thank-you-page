The Visual Art League is a nonprofit, all volunteer organization established in 1978 to advance the visual arts through education, and the promotion of art for the enrichment of the individual and the community. Meetings and events are held in the LEWISVILLE GRAND THEATER building in old town Lewisville.


VAL general meetings are the second Tuesday of each month at 7pm.
